About

CHEMTRIX'23 is a highly anticipated and prestigious gathering that brings together students, faculty, and industry experts to engage in insightful discussions and knowledge sharing. This annual event serves as a platform for intellectual growth, where participants can explore emerging trends . With a diverse range of keynote speeches, and interactive sessions, the CHEMTRIX'23 fosters a stimulating environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and networking opportunities. Students gain invaluable exposure to cutting-edge advancements in this field, while also showcasing their own talents and accomplishments. Overall, the CHEMTRIX'23 is a golden opportunity in enriching experience that contributes to the academic and professional development of everyone involved.

Your're cordly invited!!

Join us in CHEMTRIX'23, a National level Technical Symposium. This symposium is organized by the Department of Chemical Engineering, St.Joseph's College of Engineering, on the 2nd of September 2023 from 8am to 3pm. Come, learn everything about research, development and recent trends in chemical engineering, from the industry's top professionals and also even get a chance to prove your worth to them!!!

Chem-E-Cipher Rules

×
  • 3 members per team.
  • Round 1: Decoding (You will be given with atomic and mass numbers and you should decode the numbers and find the compound or elements).

  • Round 3: Puzzle (related to chemical engineering). Winners based on overall score in all three rounds
